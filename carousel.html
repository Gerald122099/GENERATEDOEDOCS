Complete Documentary Requirements

Issuance of Legal Clearance

Issuance of Official Receipt

Step 6

DTS

With the submission of the documentary requirements, issuance of DOE Clearance of No Pending Case and payment of required fees, the application shall be turned over to the assigned Processor Staff after logging.

RESPONSIBLE PERSON: EIMD
1 Day

Step 7

Processing of Application

3. Upon submission of requirements, payment, and logging, the application is assigned to the Processor Staff.
Assess completeness, format, validity, and compliance with DOE Circular DC2017-11-0011 and related standards.
4.b Register the application in the OIMS DOI Database.
4.c If compliant, prepare the Validation Form and Checklist of Requirements.
4.d If non-compliant, return to the applicant with reasons for rejection.

RESPONSIBLE PERSON: EIMD
10 Days

Step 8

Review and Endorsement

5.a The Chief SRS/Supervising SRS reviews the application and endorses the Certificate issuance, forwarding the validation form to the Director.
Insufficient applications are returned to the Assessor for applicant notification, while other documents go to the Assigned Staff for filing.

RESPONSIBLE PERSON: CARIDO, W. M.
5 Days

Step 9

Approval of Application

6.a The Director approves the application on the Validation Form; disapproved applications are returned to the Chief SRS with reasons.
6.bApproved forms go back to the Assigned Staff.
